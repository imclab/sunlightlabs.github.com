House of Representatives - Roll Call votes

The Clerk of the House of Representatives publishes roll call votes in XML, within minutes of the vote being taken. The latest votes are listed by year (e.g. 2012) on HTML pages, which link to XML files for individual votes (e.g. 2012 Roll #126).

We gather this data to serve the Real Time Congress API, which in turn serves our products and mobile apps.

Here, you can download zip files containing all roll call votes for a given year, back to 1990.
